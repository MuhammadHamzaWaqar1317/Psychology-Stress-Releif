Hatha Yoga

Hatha yoga is a traditional form of yoga that focuses on physical postures and breath control.

Vinyasa Flow

Vinyasa flow yoga emphasizes the coordination of movement and breath in a continuous flow.

Power Yoga

Power yoga is a dynamic and intense form of yoga that combines strength, flexibility, and cardio.

Restorative Yoga

Restorative yoga enables deep relaxation as you holding poses for longer periods of time with the help of props to completely support you.
